## Headline Improvement Checklist

Before finalizing, verify your winner:

- [ ] Primary keyword included
- [ ] Under 60 characters
- [ ] Would work as email subject line
- [ ] Works without any context
- [ ] Content actually delivers on promise
- [ ] Not clickbait—genuine value
- [ ] Unique from competitor headlines
- [ ] Uses active voice
- [ ] Scored 35+ out of 60
