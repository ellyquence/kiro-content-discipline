## Voice Checklist

When reviewing content, check:

- [ ] Matches our formality level
- [ ] Energy/enthusiasm appropriate
- [ ] Humor (if any) fits our style
- [ ] Authority positioning correct
- [ ] No off-brand words or phrases
- [ ] Tone matches content type
- [ ] Would sound natural read aloud
- [ ] Consistent throughout piece
